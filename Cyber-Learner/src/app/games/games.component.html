Team Work For Students

Cybersecurity Case Studies:

Description: Present real-world cybersecurity incidents or breaches and have teams analyze them. Each team can focus on a different aspect like the attack vector, mitigation strategies, and lessons learned.

Benefits: Encourages critical thinking, research skills, and understanding of real-world security incidents.

Security Awareness Campaigns:

Description: Have teams create and execute a security awareness campaign for their friends. This could include presentations, posters, and workshops on topics like password security, phishing awareness, and safe online behavior.

Benefits: Fosters communication skills, teamwork, and educates the wider school community about cybersecurity.
