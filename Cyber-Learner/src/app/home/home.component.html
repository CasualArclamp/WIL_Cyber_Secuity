Introduction

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks. It is also known as information technology security or electronic information security. The term applies to a variety of contexts, from business to mobile computing, and can be divided into several common categories.

Main categories

  • Network security
  • Application security
  • Information security
  • Operational security
  • Disaster recovery and business continuity
