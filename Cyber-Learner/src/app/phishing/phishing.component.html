What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cybercrime in which one or more targets are contacted by email, phone, or text message by someone pretending to be a legitimate authority to lure individuals into providing sensitive data such as personally identifiable information, bank and credit card details, and passwords.

Common features of Phishing Emails

  • 1. Reciprocity
  • 2. Commitment
  • 3. Social proof
  • 4. Liking
  • 5. Authority
  • 6. Scarity

    • Common features of Phishing Message

  • 1. Unknown / personal phone number
  • 2. Generic greetings
  • 3. Unusual URL
  • 4. Spelling and grammar errors
  • 5. Lack of contact information
  • 6. Request for sensitive information (credit card information)

    • How to prevent Phishing attacks?

  • Do not trust any messages from unknown phone numbers
  • Check URL before click in
